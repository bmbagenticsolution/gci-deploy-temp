About Gulf Capital Intelligence

DIFC-registered investment screening intelligence for capital allocators evaluating UAE and GCC opportunities.

What GCI Is

Structured screening intelligence for GCC capital decisions.

Gulf Capital Intelligence produces conviction reports on specific investment opportunities across the UAE and GCC. Each report covers 10 sections, delivers a clear PROCEED / PROCEED WITH CONDITIONS / AVOID verdict, and includes three financial scenarios, regulatory flags, risk matrix, and required diligence actions.

GCI is not a substitute for full due diligence. It is the structured first-pass screen that tells you whether a deal deserves deeper work.

What GCI Is Not

Clear boundaries on what this platform does and does not do.

GCI is not regulated investment advice. It is not a financial model. It is not legal or tax counsel. All return figures in GCI reports are directional estimates. Regulatory and tax positions require verification with qualified UAE advisors before any capital commitment.

GCI Conviction Engine systematic intelligence is designed to give capital allocators a structured first-pass view. It does not replace professional advisory services. It complements them.
