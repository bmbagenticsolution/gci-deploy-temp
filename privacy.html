Who We Are

Gulf Capital Intelligence is operated by Boost My Business AI Innovation Limited, registered at DIFC Innovation Hub, Dubai International Financial Centre, Dubai, UAE. Trade Licence CL11954.

What Data We Collect

  • Account information: name and email address provided at registration
  • Investment brief data: investment type, sector, geography, ticket size, and deal-specific information you enter into the platform
  • Uploaded documents: financial statements, information memoranda, or other files you upload for auto-extraction
  • Usage data: pages visited, reports generated, session duration
  • Payment information: processed by Stripe. We do not store card details on our servers.

How We Use Your Data

  • To generate investment intelligence reports based on your brief
  • To process payments and maintain your account
  • To communicate service updates

We do not use your data to train language models. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing or analytics. Anonymised usage metrics (pageviews, session counts) are used for internal platform operations only.

Uploaded Documents

Documents you upload for auto-extraction are processed to identify deal-relevant data fields. Uploaded content is transmitted to our AI processing providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, and Google) under their respective enterprise API terms solely to generate your report. We do not permanently store your uploaded documents beyond the active session. We do not use uploaded documents to train language models or share them with third parties. GCI does not opt into any model training programs offered by API providers.

Third-Party Subprocessors

  • Anthropic (AI processing) - United States
  • OpenAI (AI processing) - United States
  • Google (AI processing) - United States
  • Stripe (payment processing) - United States
  • Vercel (hosting and infrastructure) - United States
  • ElevenLabs (voice synthesis) - United States

Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. Brief content and uploaded documents are not retained beyond the active session unless you explicitly save a report to your report history. Saved reports can be deleted from your history at any time. You may request deletion of your full account and associated data by contacting us at difc@gulfcapitalintelligence.com.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at difc@gulfcapitalintelligence.com to exercise these rights.
