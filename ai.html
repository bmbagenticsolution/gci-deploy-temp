ARIA Voice Concierge

ARIA is the bilingual English/Arabic voice AI concierge embedded on every page of gulfcapitalintelligence.com. Click the voice icon (bottom right corner) on any page and ask a question out loud. ARIA responds in real time with synthesised voice in the same language you asked in.

How ARIA is built

Reasoning engine: Azure OpenAI gpt-4o. Voice synthesis: Azure Speech Services with neural voices (en-US-JennyMultilingualNeural for English, ar-SA-ZariyahNeural for Arabic). Context: persistent session memory via Cosmos DB (50 turns). Infrastructure: Azure UAE North for data residency compliance.

What makes ARIA different

Bilingual detection. ARIA automatically detects whether you are speaking English or Arabic and responds in the same language. She handles seamless code-switching mid-conversation.

Trained on GCI methodology. ARIA is instructed to behave as a voice interface to the Conviction Engine framework. Not a generic AI assistant.

Real-time voice, not text-to-speech of a chat window. Voice-first interaction, optimised for mobile conversation while commuting through Dubai traffic or walking DIFC.

Data residency. ARIA conversations are processed and stored in Azure UAE North, compliant with DIFC Data Protection Law 2020.

FAQ

Is the GCI GPT really free?

Yes. Free to use inside ChatGPT. You need a ChatGPT account (free or paid). Usage is governed by ChatGPT's rate limits, not GCI's.

Does ARIA record my voice?

ARIA transcribes your voice input to text for processing by the reasoning engine. Conversations are stored in session memory (Cosmos DB) for up to 50 conversational turns to provide continuity within a visit. Session data is subject to GCI's privacy policy at /privacy.html.

Are these AI tools investment advice?

No. Neither the GCI GPT nor ARIA provides regulated investment advice. They provide investment screening intelligence aligned with the public GCI Conviction Engine methodology. For decisions involving capital commitment, order a full Conviction Report and seek qualified counsel in your jurisdiction.

Can I use the GCI GPT in Arabic?

Yes. If you write to the GCI GPT in Arabic, it responds in Arabic. ARIA similarly responds in whichever language you use.

How do you update the AI assistants with new methodology?

The GCI GPT knowledge base is updated when new material Conviction Reports, methodology notes, or sector insights are published. ARIA system instructions are versioned alongside the website.
