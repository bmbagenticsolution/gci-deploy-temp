Connect LinkedIn

To sync job posts automatically from your LinkedIn company page, connect your LinkedIn account. You will need a LinkedIn App with the correct permissions (see setup guide below).

LinkedIn App Setup (3 steps): 1. Go to linkedin.com/developers/apps/new and create a new app.
2. Under Products, add Sign In with LinkedIn and Share on LinkedIn. For job posting access, also request Marketing Developer Platform.
3. Set the following Vercel environment variables:
LINKEDIN_CLIENT_ID = your_app_client_id
LINKEDIN_CLIENT_SECRET = your_app_client_secret
LINKEDIN_ORG_ID = your_company_numeric_id
LINKEDIN_REDIRECT_URI = https://gulfcapitalintelligence.com/api/linkedin-jobs?action=oauth-callback Get your company numeric ID from your LinkedIn company page URL or the API.
Webhook URL (for Easy Apply): https://gulfcapitalintelligence.com/api/linkedin-jobs?action=webhook
