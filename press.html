About Gulf Capital Intelligence

Gulf Capital Intelligence (GCI) is a DIFC-registered investment intelligence platform that produces independent Conviction Reports for capital allocators evaluating UAE and GCC opportunities. Every report delivers one of three verdicts: PROCEED, PROCEED WITH CONDITIONS, or AVOID. The GCI Conviction Engine combines a 5-stage analytical pipeline with three independent AI engines (Claude Opus 4.7, GPT-4.1, Gemini 2.5 Pro) and a 5-tier evidence system. Reports from USD 499.
